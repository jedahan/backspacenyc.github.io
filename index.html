backspace.nyc

backspace.nyc is a collection of creative people, sharing a brooklyn studio space + gallery + storefront

backspace provides room to find your voice, solace to focus on creating, and a platform to experiment for sustainable practices

this is a place for people to figure out how to maximize the amount of time they spend doing what they love
